How it works

Three steps from idea to raining fees.

01

Deploy through pons

Fixed 1B supply, locked Uniswap V3 pool, 0.0005 ETH fee. Your wallet signs launchToken() on the factory directly.

02

Pick the fee route

Keep the fees, or hand them to a vault with rules attached: holder drops, splits or burns. Set once in the launch form.

03

The bot does the rest

Fees are claimed from the locker and distributed on your schedule. Every payout is a plain transfer, public on the token page.

Fee modes

Choose who eats.

Keep it

Fees to your own wallet. The default pons behavior, zero PonsDrop involvement.

Drop to holders trench mode

Holders #1 to #1000, up to 30 rounds, cap ladder ×2 or +$X, timer rounds if you prefer. Holding literally pays.

Split fees

Up to 20 wallets, percentages you set, paid out automatically as fees come in.

Buyback and burn

Fees market-buy the token on its own pool and go to the dead address. Supply only goes down.

Donate to charity

Fees are converted to ETH and donated to a charity of your choice. Every donation is on-chain and public.
