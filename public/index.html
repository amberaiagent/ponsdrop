How it works

01

Deploy through pons

Fixed 1B supply, locked Uniswap V3 pool, 0.0005 ETH fee. Your wallet signs, the factory deploys. Same launch as pons, plus one extra choice.

02

Pick the fee route

The creator fee wallet is set at launch. Keep it, or hand it to a robindrop vault with rules attached: holder drops, splits or burns.

03

The bot does the rest

Fees are claimed from the locker and distributed by the schedule you chose. Every round is public on the token page and verifiable on-chain.

Fee modes

Keep it
Fees to your own wallet. The default pons behavior, zero robindrop involvement.
Drop to holders trench mode
Holders #1 to #1000, up to 30 rounds, cap ladder x2 or +$X, timer rounds if you prefer. Holding literally pays.
Team split
Up to 20 wallets, percentages you set, paid out automatically as fees come in.
Buyback and burn
Fees market-buy the token and send it to 0xdead. Number of tokens only goes down.

No cap, just drops.
