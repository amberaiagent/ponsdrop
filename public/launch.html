Pick a charity

Token-side fees are sold into your pool, everything is donated as ETH. Built-in addresses are copied from each charity's own official donation page, sources linked.

Many charities (St. Jude, Save the Children, GiveDirectly) take crypto through The Giving Block or Endaoment, which generates an address per donation. Get an address from the charity's own site and paste it here. Triple-check it: donations are irreversible.
